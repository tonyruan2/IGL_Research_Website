Model Information

This model shows the probability of achieving at least the desired probability with a certain trial count.

The points on the graph are colored according to their trial count mod B, where (A / B) is the desired probability in simplest terms. Points with trial counts that are equivalent mod B have the same color.

When B is less than or equal to 8, preset colors are used. Otherwise, random colors are generated for each group of values equivalent mod B.
