Organization

The Illinois Geometry Lab provides opportunities for mathematical research to undergraduate students attending the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Undergraduate students work in groups of 3 to 5 and are guided by graduate mentors and a faculty member.

In the fall of 2019, over 50 undergraduate students worked on projects as a part of the Illinois Geometry Lab.
